About Fairy Meadows & Nanga Parbat

  • Nestled in the heart of Pakistan's Himalayan paradise, Fairy Meadows is a pristine alpine meadow that feels like a page torn from a fairytale.
  • At the foot of Nanga Parbat, the world's ninth-highest mountain, Fairy Meadows invites you to step into a realm of natural wonder, where enchantment and adventure collide.
  • Hidden away in the Gilgit-Baltistan region, Fairy Meadows is a gem for those who seek the extraordinary. This meadow, blanketed in wildflowers, unfolds like a dream.
  • Far from the hustle and bustle of city life, Fairy Meadows offers a serene escape, where the only soundtrack is the whispering wind and the only view is a magnificent mountain.
  • Imagine waking up to the sight of Nanga Parbat, also known as 'The Killer Mountain, looming majestically above you. Fairy Meadows is your gateway to this awe-inspiring experience.
