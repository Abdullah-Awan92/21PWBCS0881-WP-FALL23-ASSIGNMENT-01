    Peshawar

  • Peshawar is a vibrant city with a rich history. Don't miss the historic Bala Hisar Fort and explore the bustling Qissa Khwani Bazaar. If yo want to eat somethinh special in peshawqar do visit Namak mandi.

    Abbottabad

  • Abbottabad is known for its beautiful natural surroundings. Visit the Ilyasi Mosque and enjoy the scenic beauty of Shimla Hill.

    Haripur Waterfall

  • Haripur noori Waterfall is a hidden gem. It's a perfect spot for a day trip, offering a refreshing escape from the city. Having Crystal Clear Water Having alot of enjoyment with Friends or family

    Mansehra

  • Mansehra is known for its lush green valleys and mountains. Don't forget to explore the Shinkiari Fort and Kaghan Valley.

    Naran

  • Naran is a popular tourist destination. Visit Saif-ul-Mulook Lake and enjoy the breathtaking views of Kaghan Valley.

    Babusar Top

  • Babusar Top is a high mountain pass connecting Naran to Chilas. Experience the stunning vistas of the Himalayas here. Best pictures spot.

    Fairy Meadows

  • Fairy Meadows is a picturesque destination at the base of Nanga Parbat. It's a paradise for trekkers and nature lovers. Do stay in camps. And visit Nanga parbat Base camp.

Local Tips

  • Try the local cuisine, especially the famous Peshawari kebabs.
  • Respect the local culture and traditions when visiting these areas.
  • Check the weather conditions before your trip, as it can vary greatly by season.
  • Always carry essential items like water, first-aid, and warm clothing for mountain trips.
